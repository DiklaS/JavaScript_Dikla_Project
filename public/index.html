Young Art- About us

Who we are?

On our website we offer real and original art at reasonable prices. In addition, we give an opportunity to young artists who are looking for a place to expose their paintings and also sell them.

How it works?

We have 3 display options for the collection of paintings displayed on our website:

Slider Display

Cards Display

List Display

Inside the card you can perform the following actions:
  • See more detalis (by clicking on the image)
  • Buy (Not available yet...)
  • Edit the Picture Card (for Buisness client only)
  • Delete the Picture Card (for Buisness client only)

When?

Whenever you want real art to decorate your home, office or any suitable space. You are welcome to sign up.
If you want to expose or sell your paintings, you are welcome to join as a business client.

Why?

Because we love art and want to make people feel good!!!
